h1. 2012 Riverflow Geomorphology

h2. This dataset is available for download and as a WMS.  

WMS Endpoint: "ogc.gina.alaska.edu/aea/suwa":http://ogc.gina.alaska.edu/aea/suwa 

"Download":http://gis.suhydro.org/pub/2012_Riverflow/  

Please contact mitch@gina.alaska.edu if you are interested in a copy via hard disk.

Quick overview of the data:

* July and September collections
* 8bit orthorectified color infrared and true color imagery 
* 1 foot resolution
* public domain license
